ABOUT MENTAL HEALTH

"Your illness is not your identity.Your chemistry is not your character."-Ricken Warren

Mental Health is a state of well-being in which people think,feel ,and behave.Mental health can affect daily living,relationships ,and physical health.

I believe we all need to know that mental health isn't a sign of weakness,shameful,all in your mind ,Always something negative ,something you can snap out of,but all in all it is not something you decide to have nor does it define who we are and who we become.

Helping Hearts Theraphy Aid is located in Johannesburg ,South Africa .HHTA is there to help teenagers suffering from mental health.

"You don't have to control your thoughts.You just have to stop letting them control you"-Dan Millman

Founder:Makhosazana Ngwenya(living with mental health)

Who Is Welcome: All teenagers,Boys and Girls

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 8pm.

TYPES OF Mental HEALTH ISSUES AND PROBLEMS/SICKNESSES

Depression,Anxiety,Eating Disorder ,Borderline personality disorder,etc.


Depression

feelings of severe despondency and dejection.


Anxiety

A feeling of worry ,nervousness,or unease about something witha certain outcome.


Eating disorder

Abnormal or disturbed eating Habits.


Borderline personality disorder

Having different personalities overtaking one's body when dealing with different situations.
